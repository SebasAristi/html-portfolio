My top 3 Movies of all-time

American Gangster

American Gangster" is a 2007 crime film directed by Ridley Scott. The movie is based on the true story of Frank Lucas, a notorious drug lord in Harlem during the 1970s. Denzel Washington stars as Lucas, who rises to power by smuggling heroin from Southeast Asia in the coffins of American soldiers returning from the Vietnam War. Russell Crowe plays Richie Roberts, the detective determined to bring Lucas to justice. The film explores themes of crime, corruption, and the complexities of the criminal justice system. "American Gangster" received critical acclaim for its performances, direction, and the gripping portrayal of a true-life criminal saga.

Jason bourne

Jason Bourne" is a 2016 action thriller film directed by Paul Greengrass. It is the fifth installment in the Bourne film series and stars Matt Damon as the titular character, Jason Bourne. The movie follows Bourne as he resurfaces, once again, to uncover hidden truths about his past. As he navigates a web of government conspiracies and high-stakes espionage, Bourne finds himself entangled in a chase that spans across various international locations. The film is known for its intense action sequences, suspenseful plot, and Damon's compelling portrayal of the skilled and determined former CIA operative.

The Pursuit of Happyness

"The Pursuit of Happyness" is a 2006 biographical drama film directed by Gabriele Muccino. Starring Will Smith and his real-life son Jaden Smith, the film tells the inspiring true story of Chris Gardner. Gardner, a struggling salesman facing financial hardship, becomes homeless with his young son. Despite numerous challenges, Gardner perseveres through a competitive internship program at a stock brokerage firm in hopes of securing a better future for himself and his son. The film explores themes of determination, resilience, and the pursuit of one's dreams against all odds, ultimately delivering a heartfelt and uplifting narrative.
